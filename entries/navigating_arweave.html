Navigating the Arweave Ecosystem [Work in progress]
Arweave is a blockchain-based platform that focuses on permanent, decentralized storage of data.

It uses a unique consensus mechanism called "Proof of Access" to secure and store data permanently on its blockchain.

Unlike traditional blockchain networks, Arweave is designed to store data for the long term, making it suitable for applications like archival, data preservation, and decentralized applications (DApps) that require persistent and tamper-proof storage.

Users pay a one-time fee to upload data, and miners are incentivized to store it indefinitely. Arweave provides a solution for creating a permanent and censorship-resistant archive of information.
